Courteney Abroad

Below are a few of my favorite photo's that I have captured while traveling. During my junior year of Undergrad, I was lucky enough to have the opportunity to study abroad. After I graduated college I again decided to go abroad where I lived in Rome, Italy. In my 24 short years of life, I have visited 15 different countries!
