College Soccer

For college, I decided to stay in Montana and play soccer for Montana State University. This was a four year school at the NCAA D2 level. Out of the 68 games played in those four years, I started a total of 66 of them. I accumulated a total of 5,214 minutes played in those four year. During that time I also earned myself numerous awards. I was named a tournament MVP my junior year and my senior year I was named Honorable Mention defender from my entire conference. Playing and playing well at a competitive college level was one of my proudest accomplishments.

Please enjoy this photo of my team from my junior year of college. Find the girl flexing and that is when you'll fine me! Also enjoy one of my highlight reels from my college days.

Professional Soccer

While playing at the college level made me extremely proud, I did not stop there! After I graduated from MSUB, I moved to Rome, Italy where I played for an academy and signed with a Professional football club. That experience was truly a dream come true!
